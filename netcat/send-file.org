#+STARTUP: content
* netcat send and recieve files

transfer files with netcat

** sending computer

replace 6881 with the port of your choice  
and make sure the port is open in your firewall

*** no -w option for nixos

#+begin_src sh
nc -lv 6881 < infile.txt
#+end_src

*** -w option on sender for debian

#+begin_src sh
nc -lv -w 3 6881 < infile.txt
#+end_src

*** recieving computer

replace 192.168.1.3 with the ip address of the sending computer

*** -w option for nixos

#+begin_src sh
nc -w 3 192.168.1.3 6881 > outfile.txt
#+end_src

*** no -w option for debian

#+begin_src sh
nc 192.168.1.3 6881 > outfile.txt
#+end_src
