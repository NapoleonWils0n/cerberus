#+STARTUP: content
* jack control

Set the options for Cadence

1) Switch the master driver to OSS

#+begin_src sh
jack_control ds oss
#+end_src

2) Set the sample rate, buffer size, and periods

#+begin_src sh
jack_control dps rate 48000
jack_control dps period 1024
jack_control dps nperiods 3
#+end_src

3) Set your specific capture and playback devices

#+begin_src sh
jack_control dps capture /dev/dsp3
jack_control dps playback /dev/dsp1
#+end_src
